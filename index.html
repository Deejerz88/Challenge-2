About Me

My name is Edward McMillan, but I go by DJ. I am a passionate and hard worker looking to enhance my skills as a Web Developer and Software Engineer. I have spent the past 10 years in the Run Specialty retail industry, working for some of the best companies all over the country. More recently, I noticed a "technology gap" at my current employer and decided to see if I could do something about it. I started with small projects that were easy to build, but that also had significant, positive impacts on the operations of the business. The more time I invested into the programming, the more I discovered my passion for it. Since then, I have built dozons of custom programs for my employer, ranging from simple to very complex. I love how fast-paced the industry is and how there always seems to be more challenging (and fun!) projects right around the corner. I'm excited to learn more and test my limits! When I'm not programming (either for work or hobby projects), you can usually find me running, bowling, rock climbing, playing volleyball, or pretty much doing anything that involes being outdoors and getting sweaty!
